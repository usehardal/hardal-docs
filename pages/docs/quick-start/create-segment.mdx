# Creating a new segments

## Step 1: Add a new website

- Log in to your account on Hardal. If you don't have, you can create a new one [here](https://app.usehardal.com/dashboard)

- Go to dashboard and click **Add Website**.

![Add a new website](https://res.cloudinary.com/raufsamestone/image/upload/v1675783872/hardal/docs/create-a-website/mkpwprmcwt7us1un1iel.png)

## Step 2: Fill Website Data

In this form, provide the necessary information for your website such as:

- `Website Name`: Your dApp or Website Name
- `Description` : Any description of your dApp or Website
- `URL` : Domain name of your dApp or Website.

<Callout>You don't have to add `https://` or `http://`</Callout>

![Fill the form](https://res.cloudinary.com/raufsamestone/image/upload/v1675784317/hardal/docs/create-a-website/m19uxkzgvyet8f5nbvas.png)

- `Event Type` : Choose your desired event type from the drop-down menu. This will determine the event types define your app or website scope for `web2` or `web3`.

![Event Type](https://res.cloudinary.com/raufsamestone/image/upload/v1675784334/hardal/docs/create-a-website/q4ypjnl2cfdd7lobsvi8.png)

<Callout>
  If you switch from Standard to Web3, your custom events will be merged.
</Callout>

## Step 3: Save

Once you have filled out all the required information, click the 'Save' button to create your website.
